THE FRONT MAN

He is the leader of the Masked Men and the enigmatic overseer of the Squid Game. As the Front Man, In-ho is very strict and ruthless to keep the order within the games and keep his underlings under control, executing the ones who break the rules and disturb the games.

Powers / Skills

Managers

Managers are the ground commanders who act on the direct orders of the Front Man. The Front Man trusts these guards the most. This is the highest rank of the staff.

Soldiers

Soldiers are the armed guards who enforce the rules of the Squid Games and elimination policy, as well as maintain its integrity, by force.

Workers

Workers are the 'civilians' of the guards. They cannot speak without permission from Managers.Their role is simply reduced into menial labour.
